SOFC Stack M100

High Power Density · Long Service Life

The MySOFC Stack M100 adopts advanced ceramic electrolyte material, with a power density of 1.2 W/cm² and a service life of more than 10,000 hours. It operates at a medium temperature of 650-750℃, which reduces the cost of component materials and improves the system stability. It is suitable for distributed power generation, micro-grid energy supply and other scenarios.

SOFC System S500

Integrated Design · Plug and Play

The MySOFC System S500 is an integrated energy supply system based on M100 stack, with a rated power of 500kW. It integrates fuel processing unit, power conversion unit and control system, supporting multiple fuels such as natural gas, biogas and hydrogen. It has the characteristics of high energy conversion efficiency (up to 65%) and low pollutant emission, which is ideal for industrial parks and commercial buildings.

SOFC Component C200

High Precision · Customizable

The MySOFC Component C200 includes cathode, anode, electrolyte and interconnect plate, which are core components of SOFC stack. Adopting advanced thin-film preparation technology and precision machining process, the components have excellent electrical conductivity and thermal stability. We provide customized services according to customer's stack design requirements, to meet the diverse needs of different application scenarios.
